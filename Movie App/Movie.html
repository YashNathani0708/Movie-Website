3 Idiots  Rating: 9.5/10

Overview


3 Idiots is a Bollywood comedy film that follows two friends, Farhan and Raju, on a quest to find their long-lost college buddy, Rancho. Rancho was a brilliant and unconventional student who challenged the rigid educational system. The film explores themes of friendship, following your dreams, and the pressure to succeed in India. It's a hilarious and heartwarming movie that won numerous awards.


Uri: The Surgical Strike  Rating: 9.3/10

Overview


Uri: The Surgical Strike is a 2019 Bollywood action film based on the true story of the 2016 Indian surgical strikes against militant groups in Pakistan-occupied Kashmir. The movie follows Major Vihaan Shergill, a brave Indian Para commando, who leads the mission to avenge the death of his fellow soldiers in a terrorist attack. It's a suspenseful and patriotic film that celebrates the courage and sacrifice of the Indian army.


Article 370  Rating: 10/10

Overview


Article 370 is a 2024 Bollywood political action thriller. Set against the backdrop of the 2019 abrogation of Article 370, which granted special status to Jammu and Kashmir, the film follows special agent Zooni Haksar. Tasked with a secret mission, she navigates a conflict-ridden region to quell violence surrounding this controversial political decision.


Kashmir Files  Rating:10/10

Overview


The Kashmir Files (2022) is a controversial Bollywood drama. It centers on the exodus of Kashmiri Pandits, a Hindu minority community, from Kashmir in 1990 during a violent insurgency. The film portrays their plight and the events leading up to it as a genocide. It has been criticized for historical inaccuracies and inflaming religious tensions, but praised for bringing attention to a lesser-known aspect of Kashmir's history.


My Fault   Rating: 8/10

Overview


My Fault is a Spanish teen crime drama with a touch of rebellion. Noah, uprooted from her life, moves into a luxurious mansion with her mom and her mom's new rich husband. There, she meets her new stepbrother, Nick. Sparks fly, but their forbidden attraction is complicated by the step-sibling dynamic. Fast cars, high society drama, and secret love add to the excitement.


Kantara  Rating: 8.7/10

Overview


Kantara is a 2022 Indian Kannada-language action thriller film steeped in folklore and tradition. It follows a fiery young man, played by Rishab Shetty, who clashes with a strict forest officer over land rights in a remote south Indian village. The film explores themes of conflict, faith, and the clash between tradition and modernity. Expect thrilling action sequences, a touch of mystery, and a deep connection to the natural world.


KGF Chapter-1  Rating: 9.1/10

Overview


K.G.F: Chapter 1 is a 2018 Kannada action film set in the 1970s. The story revolves around Rocky, a young man driven by a promise to his dying mother to claim power and wealth. He infiltrates the ruthless world of the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF), a notorious gold mine, disguised as a slave worker. His true motive: to assassinate the mine's owner. Expect brutal violence, themes of revenge and ambition, and a glimpse into the dark side of the gold mining industry.


KGF Chapter-2  Rating: 9.5/10

Overview


K.G.F: Chapter 2, released in 2022, picks up where the first film left off. Rocky, now the kingpin of the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF), faces new challenges. He's revered by the miners but feared by authorities and rivals. The film follows his fight to maintain control against threats from all sides: government forces, vengeful enemies, and powerful adversaries like Adheera, a ruthless warrior. Expect high-octane action sequences, political intrigue, and Rocky's struggle to fulfill his promise and achieve unchallenged dominance.
